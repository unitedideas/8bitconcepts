## Template 1: The Qualification Email
**Subject:** Quick question about [their inquiry]

Hi [Name],

Thanks for reaching out about [their request].

To make sure I'm the right fit, can you tell me:

1. What's your timeline for this project?
2. What's your budget range?
3. Have you worked with someone on this before?

Looking forward to hearing from you.

Best,
[Your name]

**Why it works:** Gets critical info upfront, filters out time-wasters, positions you as selective (increases perceived value).

## Template 2: The Follow-Up Sequence

**Email 1 (Day 1):** Initial response
**Email 2 (Day 3):** "Wanted to follow up..."
**Email 3 (Day 7):** "One last thought..."
**Email 4 (Day 14):** "Closing your file..."
**Email 5 (Day 30):** "Still thinking about this?"

**Email 2 Template:**
Subject: Following up on [project name]

Hi [Name],

Wanted to follow up on my email from [day].

I know you're busy, so I'll keep this short: I'm confident I can [specific result] for you within [timeframe].

Worth a 10-minute call to explore?

[Your name]

**Email 3 Template:**
Subject: One last thought on [project]

Hi [Name],

One last thought before I close your file:

Most [business type] I work with see [specific result] within [timeframe]. The key is [brief insight].

If this is still on your radar, let's talk.

[Your name]

**Why it works:** Persistent without being pushy. Most conversions happen after 5+ touches.

## Template 3: The Proposal Email
**Subject:** Proposal for [project name]

Hi [Name],

As promised, here's my proposal for [project].

**Scope:**
- [Deliverable 1]
- [Deliverable 2]
- [Deliverable 3]

**Timeline:** [X weeks]

**Investment:** $[amount]

**Next Steps:**
If this looks good, I can start [specific date]. Just reply with "APPROVED" and I'll send over the contract.

Questions? Just reply to this email.

Best,
[Your name]

**Why it works:** Clear scope, timeline, and price. Low-friction CTA (just reply "APPROVED").

## Template 5: The Re-engagement Email
**Subject:** [Name], is [pain point] still a problem?

Hi [Name],

It's been [timeframe] since we last talked about [project/pain point].

I wanted to check in: Is this still something you're looking to solve?

If yes, I've got some ideas that might help. Worth a 10-minute call?

If not, no worries - just reply "PASS" and I'll close your file.

[Your name]

**Why it works:** Gives an easy out (reduces pressure), low commitment ask (10 minutes).
