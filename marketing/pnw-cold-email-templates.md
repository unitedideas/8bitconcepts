# PNW Cold Email Templates — 8bitconcepts Consulting

**For:** Shane's direct outreach to PNW SMB owners.
**Voice rules** (per memory `user_shane_voice`): soft-direct, substance-only, no ceremony, no filler, no hype, no follow-up reply solicitations like "let me know what you think". One clear ask. No emoji.
**Goal:** Book a 30-min intro call (or, for cooler leads, the $500 diagnostic).
**Sender:** Shane personally (NOT from aidevboard / NOT from outreach@ — recipient should feel this is a one-to-one note, not a campaign).

---

## Voice + Format Rules (apply to every send)

- 110–160 words max. SMB owners read on phone; longer = unread.
- One specific reason this email is for them (industry, city, recent event)
- One reference to the research paper as proof you know what you're talking about
- One clear close: 30-min call OR $500 diagnostic. Not both in the same email.
- No "Hope this finds you well." No "Quick question." No "I'll keep this short."
- Sign as Shane personally. Real reply-to address (`hello@8bitconcepts.com` or personal).
- Subject lines: lowercase or sentence case, no clickbait, 4–7 words max.

4. Industry-specific workflow named in second paragraph (not generic)
5. Sender = Shane personal account or hello@8bitconcepts.com (NOT outreach@aidevboard — reputation tainted)

## Tracking

Replies + booked calls flow into the lead pipeline:
- If recipient fills the form → Discord "🔥🔥 NEW CONSULTING LEAD" with `source` filter
- If recipient replies directly to Shane's email → Shane logs it manually in `marketing/outreach-sent.json`

Weekly review:
- Send count
- Reply rate (target: 8–15% on personal, well-targeted PNW SMB outreach)
- Booked-call rate (target: 30–40% of replies)
- Closed-engagement rate (target: 20% of booked calls in first 90 days)

If reply rate is below 5% after 30 sends across 2+ verticals, the email format is wrong — pause and revisit.
