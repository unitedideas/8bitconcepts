## Patterns Observed

1. **Logistics in Vancouver has the best email transparency of any sector.** Both A&P Freight and Gulick publish named staff emails on their websites. Cold outreach should start here.

2. **Food/beverage producers in Portland are well-profiled** (Inc. 5000 features, press releases) but general inquiry emails (info@, hello@) are the norm. Founder names are findable but personal emails are not. Lead with the research paper hook to get above the noise.

3. **Healthcare practices hide behind contact forms universally.** LinkedIn is the only viable first-touch channel for the clinic directors and naturopathic doctors. Alternatively, Shane showing up at a Portland integrative health networking event would outperform email cold reach.

4. **Trades/HVAC is abundant but email-sparse.** Most HVAC companies use website contact forms. The ones with named owners (Ryan Albertus / Albatross, Jeremy Harmon / Tri-Tech, Sam Bright / System-Aire) were identified via LinkedIn or local news — but emails require LinkedIn DM or phone first-touch.

5. **Port of Camas-Washougal tenant directory is the single best Camas/Washougal source** — public, specific, includes addresses and phone numbers. Named owners for the smaller shops (Josh Swift, Ryan Albertus) were found via Google. This directory is worth returning to for additional Camas-area targets.

6. **Creative agencies in Portland publish general studio emails** (hello@, info@) but named founder/president outreach via those emails works — agencies are accustomed to inbound from vendors. Grady Britton's hello@ goes to a 30-person team but Paige Campbell (President) is a named decision-maker worth addressing directly.
