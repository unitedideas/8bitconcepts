## Retargeting Strategy

### Audience Segments
1. **Website visitors (no action)**
   - Ad: Social proof focused (case study)
   - Frequency cap: 3x/week

2. **Visited pricing/contact page**
   - Ad: Urgency/scarcity (limited consultation slots)
   - Frequency cap: 5x/week

3. **Email subscribers (no booking)**
   - Ad: Educational (process/how it works)
   - Frequency cap: 2x/week

4. **Past consultation (no close)**
   - Ad: New case study or testimonial
   - Frequency cap: 1x/week
