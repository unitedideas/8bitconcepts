The Problem

Pacific Home Goods had grown from a side hustle to a thriving e-commerce business doing $2.3M annually. But success brought operational chaos. Founder Marcus Chen was working 16-hour days, seven days a week, just to keep up with order processing:

  • Inventory Blindness: Shopify inventory rarely matched actual stock at their 3PL warehouse. Overselling was common, leading to refunds and angry customers.
  • Manual Order Routing: Each order required manual review to determine which warehouse (they used two 3PLs) should fulfill it based on product type, destination, and stock levels.
  • Notification Nightmares: Customer emails for order confirmations, shipping updates, and delivery notifications were cobbled together with basic Shopify templates. Customers constantly emailed "Where's my order?"
  • Returns Black Hole: Return requests came through email, Zendesk, and phone calls with no tracking system. Processing a return took 45 minutes of manual work.
  • Reorder Panic: Best-selling items would suddenly go out of stock because there was no systematic reorder point tracking. Marcus would discover stockouts when customers complained.

"During our Black Friday sale, I processed orders until 3 AM for three straight nights. My wife asked if we were actually making money or just buying ourselves a job." ‚Äî Marcus Chen, Founder

The Solution

We built a complete Order Operations Automation Platform that connects their Shopify store, two 3PL warehouses, and customer communication channels into a seamless, hands-off system.

Specific Workflows Built

1

Real-Time Inventory Sync

Hourly sync between Shopify and both 3PL warehouses via API. Stock levels update automatically, preventing oversells. Low-stock alerts trigger when items hit reorder points.

2

Smart Order Routing

AI analyzes each order's products, destination, and shipping speed requirements to automatically route to the optimal warehouse. Reduces shipping costs by 12% on average.

3

Intelligent Customer Notifications

Branded, personalized email and SMS flows: order confirmation, processing update, shipment notification with tracking, delivery confirmation, and review request. Reduced "Where's my order?" tickets by 78%.

4

Automated Reorder System

Tracks sell-through velocity and automatically generates purchase orders when stock hits predetermined thresholds. Includes supplier lead time calculations for accurate timing.

5

Returns Processing Portal

Self-service returns portal for customers. Automated return label generation, inventory restocking notifications, and refund processing. Cuts return handling time from 45 to 5 minutes.

6

Exception Management Dashboard

Central dashboard flags orders requiring human attention: address verification fails, payment issues, out-of-stock items, and shipping delays. Everything else runs automatically.
