The Problem

Nexus Creative was winning pitch after pitch, but their onboarding process was hemorrhaging deals and burning out their team. Each new client required a grueling 12-hour manual setup process spread across multiple days:

  • The Contract Chase: Proposals and contracts were sent via email. Follow-up required manual checking, calling, and reminding. Average time to signed contract: 5.3 days.
  • Information Gathering Hell: Client intake was a 47-question Google Form that clients hated filling out. Response rate was 60%, and answers were often incomplete.
  • The Tool Setup Marathon: For each client, someone had to create Slack channels, Google Drive folders, Notion workspaces, and project management boards manually. 3+ hours of copy-paste work.
  • Kickoff Coordination Chaos: Scheduling the kickoff call meant 8-10 email back-and-forths to find a time that worked for the client, account manager, strategist, and creative lead.
  • The "Did We Send That?" Problem: Welcome packets, brand questionnaires, and getting-started guides were often forgotten or sent piecemeal, creating a sloppy first impression.

"We lost a $24K project last quarter because the prospect said we seemed 'disorganized' during onboarding. They weren't wrong ‚Äî but I didn't know how to fix it without hiring a full-time ops person." ‚Äî Sarah Mitchell, Founder

The Solution

We built a Client Onboarding Automation System that transforms the "deal signed" moment into a polished, self-running experience. From contract signature to kickoff call, everything happens automatically.

Specific Workflows Built

1

Smart Contract & Proposal Flow

Proposals auto-generate from opportunity data in CRM. E-signature via PandaDoc triggers immediate Slack notification to team and welcome email to client. No more "Did they sign yet?" checking.

2

Progressive Intake System

Instead of one overwhelming form, clients receive 3 short, mobile-friendly forms over 2 days. Conditional logic shows only relevant questions. Completion rate jumped from 60% to 94%.

3

Instant Workspace Provisioning

Upon contract signature, automation creates: dedicated Slack channel, Google Drive folder structure, Notion project workspace, and Asana project template. Takes 45 seconds vs. 3 hours.

4

Intelligent Kickoff Scheduling

System checks calendars of all required team members and presents client with 3 available time slots. Once selected, invites are sent automatically with Zoom link and agenda.

5

Branded Welcome Journey

7-email welcome sequence over 10 days: immediate confirmation, team introductions, what to expect, resource library access, questionnaire reminders, and pre-kickoff prep.

6

Internal Handoff Automation

Account managers receive pre-populated briefs with all client info, goals, and preferences. Creative team gets notification with project scope and resource links. No more "What did we promise them?"
